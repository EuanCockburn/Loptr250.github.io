The Password Strength Meter



What is it?

There are many password strength meters in place today. You see them when you are registering for almost any website telling you if your password is 'weak', 'strong' or somewhere in between. But what does 'weak' or 'strong' really mean? With this thought in mind we decided to create a meter that tells you a little bit more about the passwords you create.
